Chelsea FC’s first team is back at Cobham Training Ground to prepare for the upcoming season. With the addition of new signings and a few changes to the coaching staff, head coach Mauricio Pochettino has high hopes for a strong campaign ahead.

New Signing Announced: Star Winger Joins Chelsea

September 20, 2024

Chelsea Football Club is delighted to announce the loan signing of England International winger Jadon Sancho from Manchester United. The 24-year-old has joined the Blues on a season-long loan deal for the 2024/25 campaign.

Sancho, known for his pace, dribbling skills, and creativity, expressed his excitement about joining Chelsea.

"I'm thrilled to be here at Chelsea and can't wait to get started. The club has a rich history and I'm looking forward to playing at Stamford Bridge and contributing to the team's success this season."

The loan agreement includes an option for Chelsea to make the transfer permanent at the end of the season. Sancho will wear the number 17 shirt for the Blues and is expected to provide a significant boost to Chelsea's attacking options.

Chelsea manager ENZO MARESCA welcomed the addition, saying,

"Jadon is a top-class player who will bring flair and creativity to our attack. His experience in the Premier League and at international level will be invaluable to the squad."
